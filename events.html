Events

What's Happening:

A few times a month, Beacon Street Records goes from your not so standard record store to an even less standard music venue or even a late night community hub. Check back regularly, we're always updating our schedule!

Select events are 21+ and require a purchase of ticket ahead of time. If an event is not 21+, it is free to the community so don't be afraid to visit us for one of our events!

Nov 20 - Karaoke Night




Calling all singers, non singers, and people who think they can sing but really can't. Bring a couple friends, and see who can hit those high notes! Event starts at 7pm.

Nov 26 - Thanksgiving Jam Sesh




Have you ever wanted to jam out before your Thanksgiving Dinner? Us too... That's why this year, we're making that dream a reality. Starting at 3pm, we will have an assortment of local bands in the shop to jam out while you find some records or simply hang out and listen.

Dec 1 - Late Night




We've got the coffee, tunes, and atmosphere. All we're missing is you. Starting at 9pm and going until midnight, this is our chance to embrace our inner night owls. We'll be playing some of our favorite late night albums, ranging from jazz to hip hop to classical music. Let's get sophisticated, y'all.

Calling all poets! On December 17th the store is going to become a stomping ground for Brookline's illest lyricists and wordsmiths. We'll have some instrumental beats for those wanting a backtrack, and lots of snapping for those who want strict silence. Starting at 6pm, stop by to share your own poetry or hear some of what your community has to share!
