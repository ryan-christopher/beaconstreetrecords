Beacon Street Records

33 & 1/3 times better

Boston's latest record store, recording studio, and music event space - Beacon Street Records. We strive to bring the community together in a place that lets people find music, play music, and listen to each other. Founded by music nerds, our space is the place to go to for making music, finding music, and listening to music.

Located one block from Coolidge Corner, our store is in the heart of Brookline and is easily accessible from all parts of the city. Our site has information about our store, our studio, the events we have going on, and how to get in touch with us. Stop in at our store or reach out to us online!
